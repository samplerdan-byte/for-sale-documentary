## What Is This?

**FOR SALE** is a free, fully sourced interactive documentary tracing the arc of concentrated power from the first city-states to the present day. It is built as a scrollable web experience with synchronized narration — 73 audio episodes that play as you read, or all at once in the audio player.

Every claim is drawn from court filings, congressional records, SEC/CFTC filings, FEC disclosures, and investigative journalism. Sources are linked throughout.

**The thesis:** From the Pyramids to the Pentagon, from the Dutch East India Company to FTX, from the South Sea Bubble to the Bitcoin Reserve — the tool changes. The pattern doesn't.

**Everything is for sale. The only question is whether you're buying — or being sold.**

## Featured Content

### Act I — In the Beginning
From the Big Bang to Homo Sapiens. The story of how energy concentrates, how life exploits gradients, and how the species that could tell stories inherited the Earth.

### Act II — Dominance
Rome. The Crusades. Colonialism. Tulip mania. The South Sea Bubble. World Wars I and II. Eight millennia of the same pattern: find a story that justifies taking from the many to enrich the few, and repeat until it collapses.

### Act III — The Machine Age
The computer. The microchip. The first AI hype cycle. The AI Winter. The internet. The dotcom bubble. Social media. Every transformative technology follows the same arc: military funding, commercial hype, disillusionment, quiet progress, explosion.

### Act IV — The Promise
The 2008 financial crisis. Satoshi Nakamoto. The Silk Road. The ICO boom. DeFi and NFTs. How Bitcoin was born from a genuine grievance and then became the thing it was built to replace.

### Act V — The $8 Billion Lie
Sam Bankman-Fried. The rise of FTX. The machine. Six days in November 2022. The trial. The victims. A 25-year sentence, $8.7 billion in customer losses, and a $500M investment in Anthropic — made with stolen money — that turned out to be one of the best venture bets in history.

### Act VI — Regulatory Capture
The CFTC revolving door. $100 million in donations split across the aisle. The Bahamas. Gary Gensler. How FTX hired 13 former regulators and offered to pay off a country's national debt while operating under a "light touch" framework with no requirement to segregate customer funds.

### Act VII — The Takeover
The Fairshake PAC. Congressional scalps. Pardons. The SEC swap. The GENIUS Act. How a $245 million investment in the 2024 election cycle delivered the greatest return on political capital in American history.

### Act VIII — Big Tech Pays
Facebook's rise. The 2016 election. Frances Haugen and the Facebook Papers. Meta's metaverse bet. The capitulation. The man who was once hauled before Congress now writes $1M inauguration checks and dismantles the safeguards his own employees begged him to keep.

### Act IX — Presidential Crypto
The pivot. The memecoin. World Liberty Financial. The Bitcoin Strategic Reserve. The AI arms race. A sitting president with active financial interests in the industry he is supposed to regulate.

### Act X — The Pattern
14,000 years of war profiteering. The East India Company. The military-industrial complex. 674 Pentagon officials who moved to defense contractors. The question that has no clean answer — and the one you already know.

## Technical Details

Built entirely with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks. No build tools. No tracking. Served as static files on GitHub Pages.

- Synchronized scroll-triggered narration via `IntersectionObserver`
- 73 MP3 audio segments, preloaded on demand
- Full podcast-compatible RSS feed
- ePub edition for offline reading
- PWA manifest for installation as an app
